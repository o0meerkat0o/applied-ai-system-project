Rock music is a guitar-driven genre with roots in blues and rhythm and blues from the 1950s. It spans a wide energy range from slow ballads to intense hard rock and metal. Rock listeners often seek intense or moody emotions and appreciate raw, powerful sound. Acousticness varies widely — acoustic rock exists alongside heavily distorted electric rock. Tempos typically range from 100-160 BPM depending on the sub-genre. Danceability is moderate. Rock sub-genres include alternative, indie rock, punk, metal, and classic rock. Artists like Nirvana, Led Zeppelin, and Radiohead are iconic representatives. Rock is strongly associated with live performance energy and emotional catharsis.
