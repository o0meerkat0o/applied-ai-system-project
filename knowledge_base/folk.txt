Folk music is rooted in acoustic storytelling traditions. It features acoustic guitars, simple song structures, and personal or community narratives. Energy is low to moderate, acousticness is very high, and moods tend to be relaxed, happy, or melancholic. Tempos range from 80-110 BPM. Folk listeners value authenticity and lyrical depth. Artists like Bob Dylan, Fleet Foxes, and Phoebe Bridgers represent the genre.
