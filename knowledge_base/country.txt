Country music originated in the rural American South and is characterized by acoustic and steel guitar, fiddle, and storytelling lyrics about love, loss, and everyday life. Energy is low to moderate, acousticness is high, and moods are typically relaxed or happy. Tempos sit around 80-100 BPM. Country listeners value sincerity, tradition, and emotional directness. Artists like Johnny Cash, Dolly Parton, and Morgan Wallen represent the genre across its classic and modern forms.
