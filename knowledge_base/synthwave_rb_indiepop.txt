Synthwave is a retrofuturistic electronic genre inspired by 1980s film soundtracks and video game music. It features synthesizers, driving basslines, and a moody or nostalgic atmosphere. Energy is moderate to high, acousticness is very low, and tempos range from 100-120 BPM. Listeners are drawn to its cinematic and nostalgic qualities.

R&B (Rhythm and Blues) blends soul, funk, and hip-hop influences into smooth, groove-oriented music. Energy is moderate, danceability is high, moods range from happy to moody, and acousticness is low to moderate. Tempos sit around 90-110 BPM. Artists like Beyoncé, Frank Ocean, and SZA define the modern sound.

Indie pop is a genre defined by independent production aesthetics, melodic songwriting, and an eclectic blend of pop and alternative influences. Energy ranges from low to high depending on the artist, acousticness is moderate, and moods span happy to moody. Listeners value artistic independence and emotional sincerity. Artists like Vampire Weekend, Clairo, and Sufjan Stevens represent the genre.
