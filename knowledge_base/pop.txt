Pop music is one of the most widely consumed genres globally. It is characterized by catchy melodies, verse-chorus song structures, and polished production that emphasizes clarity and accessibility. Pop listeners typically enjoy upbeat or happy moods and moderate-to-high energy levels. The genre blends elements from many other styles and tends to have high danceability and valence. Typical listeners are drawn to emotional relatability and songs that feel immediately familiar. Pop works well for social settings, commutes, and workout sessions. Artists like Taylor Swift, Dua Lipa, and The Weeknd represent the modern pop sound. Pop songs usually sit between 100-130 BPM and have low acousticness due to heavy electronic production.
